# Research Journal: Proteomics-Metabolomics Connection

## Goal
Find a novel and scientifically grounded connection between proteomics and metabolomics data available in the lab, informed by current literature.

## Plan
1. **Literature Review**: Search for recent high-impact research on proteomics-metabolomics integration (Multi-omics).
2. **Data Exploration**: Identify available biomarkers in the proteomics and metabolomics body systems.
3. **Hypothesis Generation**: Propose a novel connection based on literature and available data.
4. **Validation**: Perform statistical analysis to verify the association.
5. **Reporting**: Create a report and FINDINGS.md.
